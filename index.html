Sonic Mobility is a mobile app that lets you easily rent scooters for quick, eco-friendly transportation around the city.
Renting a scooter is simple:
  1. Download the Sonic Mobility app from your app store.
  2. Create an account and verify your identity.
  3. Locate a nearby scooter using the in-app map.
  4. Scan the QR code on the scooter to unlock it.
  5. Enjoy your ride!
